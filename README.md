# SNACC - Simply Not A Calorie Counter

> [!NOTE]
> SNACC is in early development and is not yet available on the App Store.

SNACC is an iOS application designed to help you effortlessly log meals and, over time, identify behavioral patterns in your eating routines.
Although it could be described as a meal logger, it's better to consider what SNACC is not: SNACC is not a

* calorie counter,
* fitness tracker,
* macro tracker,
* weight loss helper, or
* meal planner.

Rather, SNACC is a minimal, focused application that aims to help you identify triggers or commonalities that lead to healthy or unhealthy eating habits.
